Recipe: Cookies

I combined two other recipes and added some ingredients:
  1. Mix in a bowl:
    • 170g flour
    • 120g sugar
    • 2 tablespoons drinking chocolate powder
    • 1 package vanilla sugar
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  2. Add:
    • 120g melted butter
    • 1 egg
    • 1 ripe banana mashed with a fork
  3. Finally add:
    • 100g chocolate chips
  4. Wait a bit.
  5. Form 2 batches of 3 x 3 cookies on baking paper. Use one tablespoon of dough per cookie and keep in mind that it expands.
  6. Bake each batch for 10 to 14 minutes at 180° C.
Note: my cookies got too fluffy, maybe increasing the temperature or decreasing their size would help.
